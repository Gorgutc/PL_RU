Lucide React · 1.6 stroke · 14–18 px · color follows --text-primary or accent on hover
Icons are drawn from lucide-react at 1.6 stroke. Tint follows text color and warms to --gold-primary on hover/active. Use 14 px in dense HUD panels, 16 px in headers, 18 px in toolbar buttons. Avoid emoji and ad-hoc SVG; the only sanctioned "glyph" is the hex ⬡ mark used beside the OSIRIS wordmark.
